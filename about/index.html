I’m Terry John. Six years shipping products end-to-end, as a designer who codes and builds AI systems. Six years building the kind of products that usually need three people. Most designers stop at the handoff. I stay until it ships. That means writing the frontend, integrating the APIs, tuning the AI layer. Every decision gets made with the product’s outcome in mind, not just the spec.

Process follows the problem. I don’t bring a methodology. I bring judgment. When speed matters, I compress the cycle. When precision matters, I slow down exactly where it counts. What comes out is always the same: something that works, looks right, and holds up past the launch.
